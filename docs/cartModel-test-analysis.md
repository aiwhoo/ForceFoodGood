# cartModel Test Analysis

## Test 1 – Happy Path: Correct Total Calculation

A. What is being tested?
Validates that calculateTotal correctly multiplies price by quantity.

B. Why is this important?
Incorrect totals directly impact billing accuracy and user trust.

C. What could break if this fails?
Users may be charged incorrectly or see wrong totals in UI.

D. What type of test is this?
Business rule validation.

E. Benefit of testing?
Prevents regressions in pricing logic.
Protects revenue integrity.
